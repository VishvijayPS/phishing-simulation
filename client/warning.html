🔐 How to Identify Phishing Websites:
  • Check the website URL carefully before entering credentials.
  • Look for spelling mistakes or unusual domain names.
  • Do not click login links from suspicious emails or messages.
  • Always verify if the website uses HTTPS and a valid certificate.
  • Avoid entering passwords on unfamiliar or unexpected pages.
  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) for important accounts.
  • Report suspicious emails or websites to your organization or service provider.
